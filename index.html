Voice Logging (Microphone & Speech Recognition)

The app offers an optional voice logging feature that allows users to add fuel entries using spoken commands.

If you choose to use this feature:

  • The app will request access to your device’s microphone solely to record your voice input.
  • Your spoken input may be processed using speech recognition technology to convert it into text and automatically fill in fuel log data (such as fuel amount, unit price, total cost, and mileage).
  • Voice data may be transmitted to third-party processing services strictly for the purpose of speech-to-text conversion and AI-based data interpretation.

Voice recordings are not stored by the app after processing unless explicitly required for technical reasons. The microphone is only used while the voice logging feature is actively enabled by the user.
