Data Deletion Request

Last updated: 02 Apr 2026

If you are logged in, the preferred way to delete your FuelUp account data is to use the in-app account deletion flow available from Settings.

Delete your account in the app

  • You must be logged in to the account you want to delete.
  • The deletion flow may require re-authentication to confirm your identity.
  • An active internet connection may be required to complete backend cleanup.
  • Backend cleanup is intended to remove account-linked cloud data, including app records, subscription lifecycle state, retention records, push token records, and files stored in cloud storage under your account.

If you cannot access your account

If you cannot log in or cannot use the in-app deletion flow, contact us at fueluphelp@gmail.com. Please include enough information for us to identify the account and verify the request.

Important note about subscriptions

Deleting your FuelUp account does not automatically cancel subscriptions managed by Apple. If you subscribed through Apple, manage or cancel the subscription separately in your Apple ID subscription settings.

Local data on your device

Deleting your account is intended to remove account-linked cloud data. Local files or app data already stored on your device may remain until you remove them in the app or delete the app from the device.
